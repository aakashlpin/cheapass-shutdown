Cheapass.in has shut down.

Jun 22 2014 - 30 Sep 2017

It was a price tracking service which served over 7.5k users, sent over 660k price drop alerts and tracked over 40k products.

It was built and maintained by Aakash Goel, along with several contributions and donations from friends and users.
